Damas

In the Way of the Five-Fold Path, a Dama is a person who has attained enlightenment, but chosen to remain in the Great Wheel until all living beings have attained enlightenment alongside them. They are thought to reside in Heaven where they observe the mortal world, intervening periodically when doing so will ultimately assist mortals in freeing themselves from the wheel.

Famous Damas are worshipped as dieties in their own right, often in association with particular virtues or domains.

Priests and pious individuals of the Five-Fold Path generally take a vow promising to become Damas themselves when they personally attain enlightenment.
