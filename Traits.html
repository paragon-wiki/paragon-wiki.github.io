Traits

After you determine your character's ability scores, you can consult the following tables to generate or choose personality traits. You're recommended to take positive traits from your two highest ability scores, and one negative trait from your lowest abilitiy score. In case of a tie, you can choose which ability score to use.

Strength

d6 Positive d6 Negative
1 I'm always looking for an opportunity to show off my muscles. 1 I'm jealous and resentful when others perform feats of strength.
2 I use excessive (at times a bit painful) amounts of physical affection. 2 I assume anyone with conspicuous muscles is an idiot.
3 Every tree, rock, or hill I come across is a new climbing challenge. 3 I'll do almost anything to avoid swimming in a large body of water.
4 I love the feeling of heavy exertion. 4 I'm happy to sit back and watch others do all the physical labor.
5 I won't back down from a bar fight. Let 'em at me! 5 I drag my things on the ground, sometimes ruining them.
6 My strength affords me bravery; I'll go into the maw of hell if I must. 6 I'm meek, and easily intimidated by people who are bigger or stronger than me.

Dexterity

d6 Positive d6 Negative
1 My acrobatic tricks are sure to earn me some cheers and a free beer. 1 I constantly drop things: I've broken a few valuables in my day.
2 If I see something I want, I'll try to pocket it without anyone noticing 2 I am loathe to participate in social events that involve dancing.
3 I have an easy time escaping notice, and often wander into places where I'm not allowed. 3 My handwriting is atrocious and often illegible to others.
4 I don't need to be careful about what situations I put myself in, since I can always escape. 4 I sometimes accidentally break tools when using them.
5 I love to put my hands to good use, and I'm always ready to try and learn a new craft. 5 I've never been very sneaky, so at this point I don't even try.
6 I'm enchanted by a meticulously tied knot, a beautifully played instrument, an elegant dance. 6 I lag behind at the back of the party because I'm always tripping over roots or rocks.

Constitution

d6 Positive d6 Negative
1 I'm in good spirits even after a night of fitful sleep or a long trip on foot. 1 I have a very weak stomach; I can't stand the sight of spoiled food.
2 I'm always looking after my drunk buddies after a night of celebration. 2 Mild illnesses make me feel miserable and very irritable.
3 No bad sight or smell really seems to phase me. 3 I've always been prone to injury, and I shy away from danger as a result.
4 I've eaten food meant for dogs without any hesitation. 4 I'm embarrassed about how badly out of shape I am.
5 I'm willing to endure almost anything for the sake of accomplishing my goals. 5 I can't handle my liquor, but that doesn't stop me from trying.
6 My hands are so calloused that I can touch just about anything without flinching. 6 I despise long journeys. Can't we split it into two days instead?

Intelligence

d6 Positive d6 Negative
1 My mind is full of obscure, pointless facts. 1 I am very hesitant to try learning new things.
2 There is one subject which fascinates me, and I always steer conversations toward it. 2 I constantly forget where I've left my belongings.
3 My contingency plans have contingency plans. 3 The names of people and places escape me just as soon as I learn them.
4 I want to teach others about everything I know. 4 I constantly misuse big words that I hear others say.
5 When I talk, I usually use the most elaborate words I can think of. 5 I prefer settling arguments physically, rather than with words.
6 I pay close attention to how things are built, and how machines work 6 I doze off when people start talking about specifics.

Wisdom

d6 Positive d6 Negative
1 Neither flattering words nor the allure of riches can sway me. 1 I'm very nervous around animals, and try to avoid them.
2 I am calm and level-headed, even in completely new situations. 2 I'm far too trusting of strangers.
3 I judge others by their actions, not their words. 3 I'm prone to panic whenever I'm startled
4 I am diligent in my habits and don't often allow myself to indulge. 4 Hm? What's that? Sorry, I was lost in my own head again.
5 Animals are at ease around me, and I around them. 5 Even minor setbacks can ruin my mood
6 My attention to detail is uncanny, and there is little that can escape my notice. 6 I'm much too attached to luxury and pleasure

Charisma

d6 Positive d6 Negative
1 I'm full of amusing anecdotes, and I love telling them whenever I get the chance. 1 I mispronounce words constantly, to my great embarrassment.
2 I know what I'm worth, and I don't need people who will hold me back. 2 I hate calling attention to myself in social situations.
3 I view every person I meet as a potential ally, if I can win them over. 3 I'm a sore loser, and no one seems to like playing games with me.
4 I have a personal view that I hold very strongly; I won't compromise about it. 4 I find it hard to resist something if it's what everyone else is doing.
5 I'm a known flatterer, but hey, I'm good at it. 5 I have trouble controlling my emotions; sometimes I just explode.
6 I always play the role that I think the people around me will admire. 6 I have a habit of suddenly dropping conversations if the other person is being boring or annoying.
