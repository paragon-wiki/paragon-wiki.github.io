Cleric

Clerics are one of the 13 playable classes in the Dashao setting.

Clerics in Dashao are a diverse group, and can belong to any of the major religious traditions. Clerics who follow the teachings of Wujiao generally worship the Azure Emperor and some or all of his many attendants in Heaven, and see themselves as being tasked with bringing the mortal world in line with Heavenâ€™s will. Those who primarily follow Lixue instead focus their efforts on ancestor worship, deriving their powers through the favor of their ancestors, and striving to defend their homeland. Those who walk the Five Fold Path instead worship the saints who have attained enlightenment and now intervene in mortal affairs to guide others to enlightenment as well.

Mechanically: Clerics in Dashao are not proficient in light or medium armor. In exchange, they gain Unarmored Defense. While not wearing armor or wielding a shield, their AC is equal to 10 + their Wisdom modifier + their Dexterity modifier. If their domain would grant them proficiency in heavy armor, then they can instead benefit from a shield while using their Unarmored Defense.

Subclasses

Clerics in Dashao can use any of the domains described in the Player's Handbook. Ask your DM if you would like to use a subclass outside of those listed below:

  • Knowledge Domain
  • Life Domain
  • Light Domain
  • Nature Domain
  • Tempest Domain
  • Trickery Domain
  • War Domain
