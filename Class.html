Class

There are 13 playable classes in the Dashao setting: the 12 classes from the Player's Handbook, as well as one homebrew class. Homebrew variants are additionally used for the Ranger and Sorcerer classes to give them more defined identities.

The 13 classes are:

  • Barbarian
  • Bard
  • Cleric
  • Druid
  • Fighter
  • Monk
  • Paladin
  • Ranger
  • Rogue
  • Sorcerer
  • Warlock
  • Warsage
  • Wizard
