Backgrounds

Your Background represents the life you lead before discovering your powers as a Paragon. It helps determine what skills your character possesses and what equipment you begin the game with.

Characters in the Dashao setting choose from a modified list of backgrounds, adapted from those listed in the Player's Handbook. There are two major mechanical aspects of these backgrounds which should be pointed out:

  1. In Dashao, your character's traits and flaws are based off of their ability scores, rather than their background.
  2. The equipment you gain from your background doesn't consume any space in your inventory; it can be carried for 'free'.

The available backgrounds are:

  • Acolyte
  • Artisan
  • Criminal
  • Entertainer
  • Farmer
  • Hermit
  • Merchant
  • Noble
  • Official
  • Sage
  • Soldier
