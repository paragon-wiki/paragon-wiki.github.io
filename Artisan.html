Artisan

You are a craftsman, skilled in a particular field and accustomed to making a living through the work of your hands. While nominally part of the lower class, the wealth you have obtained in the mercantile world affords you an unusual degree of freedom and comfort.

Skill Proficiencies: Investigation, Sleight of Hand

Tool Proficiencies: One type of artisan's tools

Equipment: A set of artisan's tools (one of your choice), a small creation that you're particularly proud of, a set of traveler's clothes, and 15 strings of silver coins.

I became an artisan because:

d6  
1 I've always been crafty and good with my hands.
2 My family had me apprenticed to a master who taught me their trade.
3 As a child, I helped a skilled artisan keep a secret or complete a task, and in return I was taken on as an apprentice.
4 I take great pride in my craft, and see it more as a type of artistic expression than a vocation.
5 I learned my craft from my parents, who intended for me to take over their business.
6 As a child, I was awkward and lonely, and obsessively practiced my favorite craft in favor of interacting with other children.
