Monk

Monks are one of the 13 playable classes in the Dashao setting.

Altho they are in principle sworn to non-violence, Monks of the Five Fold Path long ago learned that there are many unscrupulous people who are willing to steal from a pilgrim. In response, some monasteries began to instruct their monks in martial arts, and eventually came to incorporate the practice of martial arts directly into their religious teachings as a form of active meditation. Monks from these traditions are able to sense and manipulate the Qi of themselves and others, disabling opponents with the lightest of touches, mending their own wounds in seconds, and effortlessly performing impossible-seeming physical feats.

Adventuring monks typically interpret their vow of non-violence broadly, and believe that actions which reduce the harm suffered by sentient creatures overall are compatible with their vows, even if they themselves involve the use of force.

Subclasses

Monks in Dashao by default can use any of the following five subclasses. Ask your DM if you would like to use a subclass which is not on this list:

  • Way of the Drunken Master
  • Way of the Four Elements (Redux)
  • Way of the Kensei
  • Way of the Open Hand
  • Way of Shadow
