Lei Chuo

Lei Chuo is the eldest son of the former Yizong Emperor, and the younger brother of Empress Zhishen. Like his elder sister, he is known to be a powerful Draconic Sorcerer. Following a failed coup which attempted to place him on the throne, he was exiled from the capital and enfoeffed as the Duke of Jing, granting him a noble estate in Hei Province, where he has lived for the past twelve years.
