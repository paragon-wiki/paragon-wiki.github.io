Paladin

Paladins are one of the 13 playable classes in the Dashao setting.

Like Monks, Paladins derive their magical powers from their own Qi. Unlike monks, however, this ability is not the result of contemplative meditation. Instead, Paladins are driven by raw emotion which far exceeds what any normal human experiences. Whether they are consumed by a thirst for vengeance, dedication to their lord, or an all-encompassing love of humanity, their sheer force of their devotion lends supernatural heft to their blade, and unshakable confidence to their voice. Virtually all paladins are adventurers, wandering the land as Xia, or knight-errants, fighting for their ideals.

Subclasses

The following subclasses are available to Paladins in the Dashao setting. Ask your DM if you would like to use a subclass beyond those listed here:

  • Oath of the Ancients
  • Oath of the Crown
  • Oath of Devotion
  • Oath of Vengeance
