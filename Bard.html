Bard

Bards are one of the 13 playable classes in the Dashao setting.

Music is of central importance to many of the religious traditions in the Dashao setting. For the followers of Lixue, music, when played according to the rules laid out in the Classic of Song, is a means of refining the soul of both the player and the listener. For followers of Wujiao, music played properly is the ultimate means of self-expression, fluid and spontaneous, bringing the musician in harmony with nature. Elite practitioners of either school are able to use their music to sway hearts, conjure illusions, and heal the wounded. Bards who prefer the tradition of Lixue typically belong to the College of Lore, while Bards who prefer the tradition of Wujiao typically belong to the College of Valor.

Mechanically: Bards in Dashao can use either Intelligence or Charisma as their spellcasting ability, depending on whether their style of music is more formalistic (like that of the followers of Lixue), or more improvisatory (like that of the followers of Wujiao). If a player chooses to use Intelligence as their spellcasting ability, then they become proficient in Intelligence saves instead of Charisma saves, and their uses of Bardic Inspiration are based on their Intelligence modifier.

Subclasses

Bards in Dashao typically follow one of the two colleges described in the Player's Handbook. Ask your DM if you want to use a subclass outside of the two listed below:
