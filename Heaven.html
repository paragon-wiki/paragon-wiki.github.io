Heaven

The people of Dashao see the universe as being divided into three realms, corresponding to Heaven, Earth, and People. Heaven plays several roles in this cosmology:

  • It is the afterlife to which spirits depart, and the dwelling place of ancestral spirits and deities.
  • It is a force for moral retribution, which sees to it that the wicked are punished and that the virtuous find success.
  • It is a vast bureaucracy, headed by the Azure Emperor, which serves as the model and justification for imperial authority on earth.
