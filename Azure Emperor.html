The Azure Emperor

The Azure Emperor is the highest divinity in the Wujiao pantheon and the ruler of Heaven. He is believed to be the progenitor of Tong culture, and is worshipped as a common ancestor by all Tong people. Temples and shrines in his honor can be found in every corner of the land, but the largest by far resides in the capital, used by the imperial family on each solstice in a day-long public ritual dedicated to his worship.
