Entertainer

You thrive in front of an audience. You know how to entrance them, entertain them, and even inspire them. Your poetries can stir the hearts of those who hear you, awakening grief or joy, laughter or anger. Your music raises their spirits or captures their sorrow. Your dance steps captivate, your humor cuts to the quick. Whatever techniques you use, your art is your life.

Skill Proficiencies: Acrobatics, Performance

Tool Proficiencies: Disguise kit, one type of musical instrument

Equipment: A musical instrument (one of your choice), the favor of an admirer (love letter, lock of hair, or trinket), a costume, and 15 strings of silver coins.

I became an entertainer because:

d6  
1 My once-noble family lost its title, and we were forced to scrape out a living using our musical talents.
2 I live for the thrill of being the center of attention.
3 I ran away from home to join an opera troupe.
4 Music is the only way that I can express my true feelings.
5 I earned my coin by performing on street corners and eventually made a name for myself.
6 By working as a private tutor for noble scions, I was able to taste the life of the upper class.
