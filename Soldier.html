Soldier

War has been your life for as long as you care to remember. You trained as a youth, studied the use of weapons and armor, and learned basic survival techniques, including how to stay alive on the battlefield. You might have been part of the Imperial army or a mercenary company, or perhaps a member of a local militia who rose to prominence during a recent war.

Skill Proficiencies: Athletics, Intimidation

Equipment: An insignia of rank, a trophy taken from a fallen enemy, a set of dice or a deck of cards, a set of common clothes, and 10 strings of silver coins.

Languages: One of your choice.

I became a soldier because:

d6  
1 I joined the militia to help protect my community from monsters.
2 An ancestor of mine was a soldier, and I wanted to carry on the family tradition.
3 I was forced to enlist in the Imperial army.
4 I grew up on the frontier, where fighting off bandits and barbarians was simply a reality of life.
5 I wanted fame and fortune, so I joined a mercenary company, selling my sword to the highest bidder.
6 When the White Dragon rebellion broke out, I felt it was my duty to take up arms in support of the cause I believed in.
