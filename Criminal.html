Criminal

You are an experienced criminal with a history of breaking the law. You have spent a lot of time with other criminals and still have contacts within the criminal underworld. Whether you were a conman, a bandit, a fence, or just a village bully, you are far closer than most people to the world of murder, theft and violence that pervades the underbelly of civilization.

Skill Proficiencies: Intimidation, Stealth

Equipment: A small knife, a set of dark common clothes with a hood, and 15 strings of silver coins.

Languages: One of your choice.

I became a criminal because:

d6  
1 I resented authority in my younger days, and saw a life of crime as the best way to fight against the corruption of government officials.
2 Necessity forced me to take up the life, since it was the only way I could survive.
3 An incompetent magistrate had me tattooed and exiled for a crime I didn't commit. Afterwards, criminals were the only people willing to take me in.
4 I fell in with a gang of reprobates and ne'er-do-wells, and I learned my specialty from them.
5 A parent or relative taught me my criminal specialty to prepare me for the family business.
6 The world is a cruel place, and I believed that I had to do everything I could to provide for myself and those I loved.
