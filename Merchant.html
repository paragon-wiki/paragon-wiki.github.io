Merchant

You are a trader, making your living by traveling across the land and porting goods from those who make them to those who need them. You are worldly, charming, and always ready to strike a deal. You might have operated independently as a peddler or a specialist in one particular type of merchandise, or you might have worked as an employee of a larger mercantile company.

While some merchants manage to attain considerable wealth, a certain stigma surrounds the members of your class, who are stereotypically seen as greedy, unproductive and disloyal. You should consider how this stereotype shapes the way that your character thinks of themselves, and whether they embrace it or try to fight against it.

Skill Proficiencies: Deception, Persuasion

Equipment: A handful of mostly worthless trinkets, a scale for weighing silver, a set of common clothes, and a 15 strings of silver coins.

Languages: One of your choice.

I became a merchant because:

d6  
1 I wanted to see the world without being tied down to any particular place
2 My ancestors have all been merchants for generation, and I wanted to honor their legacy.
3 After witnessing the stark poverty of a small village, I made it my mission to bring the comforts of the city to the disadvantaged.
4 I dreamt of riches, but had no appetite for hard labor.
5 Meeting new people and haggling over prices brings me joy.
6 I was exiled from my home, and had to make ends meet as an itinerant peddler.
