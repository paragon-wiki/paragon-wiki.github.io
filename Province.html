Province

Provinces are the largest administrative division in Dashao. Each province is presided over by a Governor, who is centrally appointed by the Empress. Provinces are subdivided into counties, which are in turn broken up into townships.

The past several decades have seen the influence of the central government steadily declining, with the result that, in practice, most provinces are ruled as semi-independent nations in their own right. This trend has been greatly accelerated following the defeat of the imperial army by the forces of the White Dragon Rebellion during the 11th year of the Era of Overflowing Compassion, after which the Empress issued an edict giving governors permission to raise private armies to suppress the rebellion.

There are a total of 10 provinces in Dashao, as well as the capital, which is ruled directly by the Empress:

  • An Province
  • Lu Province
  • Jiang Province
  • Jin Province
  • Kuan Province
  • Qing Province
  • Ji Province
  • Yun Province
  • Hei Province
  • Hu Province
